One Pot Creamy Cajun Chicken Pasta Recipe


Ingrediants for Cajun Seasoning Blend

  • 3 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 3/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 3/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper - or more if you like spice
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 11/2 tsp salt

Ingrediants for Chicken Pasta

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 11/2 tbsp butter
  • 1.5 lb chicken breast
  • 1 box penne pasta
  • 4oz cream cheese
  • 3.5 cups chicken broth
  • 15oz can of fire roasted tomatoes
  • 1.5 yellow onions (2 cups diced)

Directions

  1. Combine Cajun Seasonings into small container
  2. Dice onions and set to the side
  3. Cut chicken into 1/2 or 3/4 cubes. Place cubes in a bowl and toss them with the seasoning
  4. Add oil and butter to a skillet. Heat over medium-high until foamy
  5. Add chicken, cook for a couple of minutes on each side, or just until the outside gets some color
  6. Add the onions to the skillet and saute until the onions begin to soften, stirring occassionally
  7. Add the pasta, can of tomatoes with jucies, and chicken broth to the skillet. Stir to combine, place a lid on the skillet and let broth come to a boil
  8. Once boiling, turn heat down to medium-low and simmer for roughly 10 minutes, or until the pasta is tender and liquid is thickening and saucy. Stir every few minutes
  9. Turn off heat, add in cream cheese and stir to melt and create a sauce.
    • BONUS TIP: if you want it to be extra creamy add in 1/4 - 1/3 cup of heavy whipping cream
