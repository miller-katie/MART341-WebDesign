Donations

We have small jars avaliable in our offie where in person funds may be donated to help keep our small farm running if you prefer to not volunteer.

Children's Hospital

We partner with UVA Children's Hospital to provide fun for critically ill children and help support the kids in honor of Autumn. 20% of our proceeds from our farm as well as 20% of the proceeds from our food truck vendors go directly to the Children's Hospital. You can also donate directly to them through their website.

Volunteering

Our favorite donation is your time! Please consider volunteering a few hours of your time to our farm. We can use help in our petting zoo, hosting events, in our office, or maintaining our grounds. To voulunteer fill out the form below. If you have another idea of how to offer your time add in in the comments of this form. We are happy to accomadte most requests.
