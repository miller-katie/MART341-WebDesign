ACTIVITIES FOR ALL

Check back often, there's something new every season!


Apple Picking

Come by our apple farm and fill a bag of fresh picked apples or just walk through the apple trees and enjoy the view. Bags are avaliable in the office and can be filled for $5 a bag. Suitable for all ages






Sunflower Maze

Our sunflower maze is one of our most popular events, you don't want to miss it! Enjoy 10 acers of mazes and prizes along the way. Throughout our maze there will be posts to find, each post containing a different gift to a resturant, shop, or small business within our community. Posts are moved every few weeks and gifts are swapped as well!





Petting Zoo

Our Petting zoo is open year round and our animals love the attention! You can purchase feed in our main office. Some of our animals also love to be brushed, ask us if your child would like to participate in this! All animals recieve a health check daily before interacting with our guests.






Food Truck Thursday

We have several food trucks come to our farm every Thursday, rain or shine, for dinner. Food trucks are avaliable from 4pm - 7pm. Come enjoy a night away from cooking and dishes!
