Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I paddleboard?

You can take your board to just about any public body of water! Purchasing an inflatable board is really key for maximum flexibility in locations. Oftentimes there are many incrediable places to board right in your backyard. P.S. Life jackets, do not have to be worn, but are required in most states to be on the board and avaliable at all times.

What weather is best?

Paddleboarding is best done on warmer sunny days. Though clouds in the sky should not steer you away either. Cloudy days and cooler weather can make for a nice day on the water as well if you aren't wanting to burn or sweat. DO NOT go out if there is storms, rain, or high winds. The windy.com app is a great place to check for winds. You can download here for Iphones, and here for Androids.

How do I find locations?

The best way to find specific spots to put your boards in is through the FREE app called GoPaddling. This app will show you well-known locations and recently found locations. All spots are put on the map from fellow paddle boarders and each spot will provide information for parking, where to launch, if you need permits, reviews on the location, and some spots even have photos! This is by far my favorite app and it is so easy to use. You can download here for Iphones, and here for Androids.

What board should I buy?

Personally, I am pro inflatable SUP all the way!! I think these boards provide so many more benefits and flexibility. You can just throw the case in your trunk and go anywhere. Among my community, the best board is by far is the iROCKER Nautical Inflatable SUP. iRocker is a well known company that offers many more boards than just the Nautical brand. If you are looking for other options id suggest visiting thier website here and exploring all options.
