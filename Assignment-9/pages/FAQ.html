S'mores Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I use any form of chocolate?

    Yes! You can use any chocolate candy you want, so long as it will melt. Some people like to use reeses cups!

  • Whats the best baking dish?

    If you dont have an oven safe skillet or prefer not to use one you can use almost anything that is oven safe and has edges.
    Other options are a regular baking pan, glass dish, or a disposable tin from the store.

  • What else can I eat this with?

    You don't have to eat this with graham crakers!
    You can use fruits, other crackers, cookies, or even just eat it with a spoon
